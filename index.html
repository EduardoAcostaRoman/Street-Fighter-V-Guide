V-Skill I & II

Perform an action unique to your character by using V-Skill. Press Medium Punch and Medium Kick at the same time to execute moves with effects that differ between characters.

V-Trigger I & II

Play the trump card that could change the course of a match by pulling the V-Trigger. Press Hard Punch and Hard Kick at the same time when your V-Gauge is at MAX to launch a powerful, character-specific action like a teleport or timed power-up. V-Triggers cost your entire V-Gauge.

V-Shift

The V-Shift battle mechanic will help you get out of tough situations at the cost of one bar of V-Gauge. When timed correctly, V-Shift can be executed at nearly any moment in a match, and will make you fully invincible to attacks, throws, and projectiles for a short time. Perform V-Shift by pressing Hard Punch and Medium Kick at the same time. Follow up with V-Shift Break by pressing Hard Punch and Medium Kick again or by simply holding both attacks down after a successful V-Shift.
